Royal Biryani

  1. 1 cup boiled basmati rice
  2. 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves
  3. salt as required
  4. 2 tablespoon refined oil
  5. 3 green cardamom
  6. 2 clove
  7. 2 onion
  8. 1 teaspoon turmeric
  9. 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  10. 1 cup hung curd
  11. 2 tablespoon coriander leaves
  12. water as required
  13. 1 tablespoon ghee
  14. 600 gm chicken
  15. 1 tablespoon garam masala powder
  16. 1 teaspoon saffron
  17. 1 tablespoon bay leaf
  18. 1 black cardamom
  19. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  20. 4 green chillies
  21. 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  22. 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  23. 1/2 tablespoon ginger
  24. 2 drops kewra
  25. 1 tablespoon rose water

Steps to Make The Royal Biryani

  1. Step 1

    2. Prepare saffron-kewra water and chop veggies
    To make a delightful chicken biryani dish firstly soak saffron
    in water to prepare saffron water (One tsp saffron can be soaked in 1/4 cup water).
    Next, mix kewra drops in water and mix well to make kewra water. Set them aside for later usage.
    Now chop the onion and coriander leaves and keep them aside.

  2. Step 2

    3. Meanwhile, heat refined oil in a deep bottomed pan.
    Once the oil is hot enough, add cumin seeds, bay leaf, green cardamom, black cardamom,
    cloves in it, and saute for about a minute.
    Then, add chopped onion to it and saute until pink. Now, add chicken
    into it with slit green chillies, turmeric, salt to taste, ginger garlic paste,
    red chilli powder and green chilli paste. Mix well all the spices and cook for 2-3 minutes.
    Then, add hung curd into it and give a mix. (Make sure the chicken is washed properly and patted dry
    before adding it to the dish)

  3. Step 3

    4. Cook biryani on low heat for 5-6 minutes
    Turn the flame to medium again and add garam masala in it along with ginger julienned,
    coriander and mint leaves. Add kewra water, rose water and saffron water in it.
    Cook till the chicken is tender. Then add 1 cup cooked rice and spread evenly.
    Then add saffron water and pour ghee over it. You can now cook the dish without the
    lid or cover it with a lid to give a dum-effect due to the steam formation.

  4. Step 4

    5. Serve hot chicken biryani with your favourite chutney or raita
    Cook for 15-20 minutes with a closed lid and garnish with 1 tbsp fried onions and coriander leaves.
    Serve hot chicken biryani with raita of your choice. Enjoy!

Tips

  • The first and foremost important thing to take care of while preparing chicken biryani recipe is, always use a heavy-bottomed pan as you would not want the chicken getting cooked.
  • The restaurant-style chicken biryani recipe uses the whole chicken in preparation and the chicken breast can dry when cooking at home. It's always suggested to use chicken thigh
  • If you want your chicken to be juicier in your biryani, do not remove the bone.
  • If your cooked rice has turned sticky, spread it on a plate and leave it for 5 minutes. The rice will be separate and fluffy again.
  • Freshly pound spices bring out the best taste in Chicken Biryani Recipe, so make sure that you dry roast all the whole spices for a good 2-3 minutes and then pound or grind them.
