Indian Style Egg Fry

Simple boiled eggs enhanced with a home-made spice paste and
a crackling tempering. This egg masala can be a great option
for dinner parties or even a casual lunch.
This is a quick and super easy egg recipe.

Ingredients

  1. Boiled Egg
  2. Chilli Powder
  3. Ground Black Pepper
  4. Turmeric
  5. Ground Coriander
  6. Salt
  7. Curry Leaves

Coking Instruction

  • Step 1

    • Heat oil in a pan. Add Chili Powder (1 Tbsp) , Ground Black Pepper (1 tsp) , Ground Turmeric
    (1 pinch) , Ground Coriander (1 tsp) , Salt (to taste) , and Curry Leaves (1 sprig) . Mix well for 5 minutes over a low flame.

  • Step 2

    • Halve the Eggs (4) and place them in the masalas. Let it fry for 1 minute.

  • Step 3

    • Flip the eggs carefully without damaging the yolk and roast for another minute.
    Switch off the flame. Yummy egg fry is ready.
